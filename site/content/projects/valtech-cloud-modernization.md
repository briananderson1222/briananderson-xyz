### Mission
Partnered with C-suite stakeholders to lead the migration of a mission-critical Credit Card portfolio from legacy Websphere and PCF environments to **OpenShift (OCP)**.

### Outcomes
- Orchestrated the transition of **6 core applications** with **zero downtime**.
- Pioneered a "Quality First" mandate by implementing **TDD** and automated **CI/CD guardrails**.
- Reduced deployment cycles from monthly releases to **multiple on-demand daily deployments**.
- Standardized enterprise-wide integration patterns using **Helm Charts** and **Kubernetes Operators**.
