### The Concept
Taking cues from popular party games, **Fill In My Blank** was a mobile game that allowed users to play with their friends to create hilarious and often inappropriate results through a combination of *Blanks* and *Fillers*.

### Timeline
**September 2012 - January 2014**

### Technical Specs
- **Native Development:** Built from the ground up using **Objective-C** (iOS) and **Java** (Android).
- **Architecture:** Managed real-time multiplayer sessions and custom data synchronization between platforms.

### Impact & Stats
- **SEO Success:** Reached **#1 in the Google Play Store** for the "Cards Against Humanity" search term.
- **Engagement:** Maintained **500 active users per day** at its peak.
- **User Base:** Successfully scaled to **over 8,000 registered users**.
